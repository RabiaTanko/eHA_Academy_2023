Spain or the Kingdom of Spain (Reino de España), is a country located in Southwestern Europe,
with parts of its territory in the Atlantic Ocean, in the Mediterranean Sea and in Africa.
It is the largest country in Southern Europe and the fourth-most populous European Union member state.
Besides being world famous for its football, Spain is also known because of its culinary art, bullfights, architecture, beaches, etc.
If you are planning to visit here, you must carry some cash in their official currency, which is Euro.
